Glossary

Clear definitions of key terms like buyer intent, lead scoring, DM funnels, and fallback mode.

Buyer Intent

Signals in comments indicating purchase readiness: questions about price, availability, shipping, or product details.

Lead Scoring

AI-powered ranking system (1-100) that evaluates comment intent, urgency, and engagement to prioritize follow-up.

DM Funnels

Automated direct message sequences that guide prospects from initial interest through qualification to conversion.

Fallback Mode

When AI confidence is low, the system routes comments to human reviewers for manual response and quality control.

Comment Intent Detection

AI classification of comments into categories: leads, questions, complaints, spam, or general engagement.

CRM Sync

Real-time integration that pushes qualified leads with tags, scores, and conversation history into your CRM platform.
